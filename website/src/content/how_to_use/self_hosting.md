## Self-hosting

You can also download our processed full planet Btrfs images if you want to self-host yourself. Details can be found on [GitHub](https://github.com/hyperknot/openfreemap).

Start by copying `config/linux_host/config.sample.jsonc` to `config/linux_host/config.jsonc`, then fill out your domain, certificate settings, and `skip_planet` preference before running the linux-host deploy script.
